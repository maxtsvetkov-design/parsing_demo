Key risks & mitigations
Flight permits
RiskInability to obtain flight permits, impacting our ability to deliver the scope on time.
MitigationA contingency plan to resolve site-selection and seeding flight restrictions so delivery continues.
Site selection: revised methodology — a new satellite-based model with previously acquired UAV data and field surveys. Reviewed and accepted by EAD for 2026.
Seeding: if flight operations cannot be obtained, manual seed planting by field workers (~2 months before activation). Manual seeding protocol with EAD for approval — ongoing.
Land availability
RiskEAD confirms contractual mangrove targets are not attainable due to land unavailability.
MitigationRe-assess attainable mangrove targets and move impacted funds into equally important Abu Dhabi ecosystems. With EAD, Nabat integrated arid ecosystem restoration and monitoring — amounts moved from mangrove seeding targets, mapping targets untouched. Approved by EAD; programme scope revision ongoing.
Arid restoration & monitoring
ScopeRestore and monitor degraded arid ecosystems — including rehabilitation of critical vegetation inside protected areas, and sustainable rangeland.
